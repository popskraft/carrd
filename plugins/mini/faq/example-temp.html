Q. Can I mix different VLT on one vehicle?

Yes. You can use carbon window tint and ceramic window tint by zones. Check local rules for VLT limits before install.

Q. Are the rolls compatible with plotter cutters?

Yes. Standard widths like 24 inch tint roll and 36 inch tint roll work with common plotters. A 60 inch master roll is used for larger panels.

Q. What does ceramic window tint add versus carbon?

Ceramic often provides higher heat control at the same VLT and a cleaner view on lighter shades. Carbon offers a lower roll cost.

Q. Is window tint film suitable for a windshield visor strip?

Yes, shops often use a lighter VLT for the top strip. Confirm what is allowed in your state or region.

Q. How do I care for PPF after installation?

Paint protection film with a hydrophobic top coat washes easily. Use mild soaps and non-abrasive materials. Avoid harsh compounds.

Q. Where does PPF make the biggest difference?

On high-impact zones: front bumper, hood edge, front fenders, rocker panels, and door edges. This helps with rock chip protection.

Q. Are the films compatible with standard install solutions?

Yes. Window tint film works with typical slip solutions or install gels. Follow the product guidance on mix ratios and surface prep.

Q. Who handles distribution and reorders?

Distribution is handled by AK Dealer Services. Specify widths and VLT when ordering to speed up processing.
